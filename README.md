# 🎵 SoundWave 
# **Android Version 10**
**A Next-Generation, Personalized Music Experience for Android.**

SoundWave is a feature-rich music player designed to bring together all your favorite music sources into one beautiful, acrylic-themed interface. Whether it's streaming from global platforms or playing your local collection, SoundWave handles it with high-fidelity audio and AI-driven insights.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🚀 Smart Streaming
*   **Multi-Source Integration**: Stream seamlessly from YouTube, JioSaavn, SoundCloud, and Spotify.
*   **Offline Mode**: Scan and play your local device music alongside online streams.
*   **AI DJ**: Get personalized recommendations based on your listening habits.

### 🎧 High-Fidelity Audio
*   **10-Band Equalizer**: Fine-tune your sound with professional presets (Bass Boost, Smooth, Clear, etc.).
*   **Crossfade**: Enjoy smooth transitions between songs.
*   **Volume Normalization**: Maintains consistent volume levels across all tracks.

### 🎨 Personalization
*   **Regional Trends**: Select your country (India, US, UK, etc.) for localized music discovery.
*   **Language Focused**: Support for 100+ artists across Hindi, Punjabi, English, Tamil, Telugu, and more.
*   **UI Customization**: Custom backgrounds, Accent colors, and the stunning **Acrylic Blur** effect.

### 🔄 Built-in Update System
*   Stay up to date without visiting a website. SoundWave features a **Direct Background Update** system that notifies you of new versions and installs them automatically.

---

## 📥 Installation

1.  Navigate to the [Releases](https://github.com/Rajat-Solankii/SoundWaveApp/releases) section.
2.  Download the latest `SoundWave.apk`.
3.  Open the APK on your Android device and follow the installation prompts.
    *   *Note: You may need to "Allow installation from unknown sources" in your device settings.*

---

## 🛠️ Update System (Internal)
This repository hosts the `update.json` file used by the app's internal update checker. 

**To push an update:**
1.  Upload a new APK to a GitHub Release.
2.  Increment the `versionCode` and `versionName` in `update.json`.
3.  Provide the new direct download link in the `updateUrl` field.
